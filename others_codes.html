If the UIU/keypad has PLC written on it, ensure it is plugged to a socket in the house using the meter.

If the UIU/keypad has RF written on it, input the recommended batteries or just plug to a powerbank.

The ENTER button is almost always on the DOWN RIGHT side of the UIU and should not be confused with the CANCEL button which is usually on the left side
